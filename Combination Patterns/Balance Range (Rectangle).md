Sideways price action with sets of equal highs and equal lows.

Only **four** possible outcomes, with clear target and entries. 

Strong on larger time frames. 30 Minute, Hourly, Daily....

![](attachments/Pasted%20image%2020250409102800.png)

1. Look **Above** & **GO**
	- Break at resistance level 
	- Go to target T1
	- Look to double range consolidated in
	- Look and monitor for green continuation candles
2. Look **Above** & **FAIL**
	- Break resistance level
	- Assume always going to Target 1
	- Come into resistance 
	- Fail back below prior resistance level
	- Target bottom end of support level
	- Monitor for continuation lower 
3. Look **Below** & **GO** 
	- Break of support
	- Target double to downside 
	- Monitor for continuation lower
4. Look **Below** & **FAIL**
	- Come above prior support
	- Target top end 
5. Remain in the balance (sideways)

## Cup and Handle / Inverted 

Has multiple components. Reversal top & bottom and stacked flag pattern. 

Price forms a rounded bottom/top, makes an advance/decline, consolidates and continues. 

Strongest on higher time frame charts. 30 Minute, Daily, Weekly, etc...
