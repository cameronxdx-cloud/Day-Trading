**Liquidity Heat Map (Deep DOM)** - Displays passive sellers and buyers (market maker liquidity) resting in the order book. Shows historical depth of market and areas with highest institutional liquidity that act as support/resistance.
Volume-Based Indicators

**VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)** - Institutional reference widely used as execution algorithm benchmark

**CVD (Cumulative Volume Delta)** - Measures who's more aggressive between buyers and sellers
