Measure s and plots the volume weighted average of selling pressure versus buying pressure.

If CMF is negative - More sellers than buyers.
If CMF is positive - More buyers than sellers.

Default settings look at the prior 21 periods. 

## Scenarios To Watch For

Best used as a confirmation indicator. 

Will help keep you in a winning trade longer, or add to the trade as it starts working. 

How high/low is the CMF?
How long has it stayed above/below 0?
How does it react on pullbacks?
