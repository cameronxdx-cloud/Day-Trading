## Lower Studies 
- These are **NOT** buy and sell signals. 
- Lagging indicators that are calculated based on price action and volume - our core foundation to trading. 
- Can **help** confirm or deny the bias that you come up with. What type of candles are forming?

- Oscillators - Range bound between two extremes.
- Can be used for recognizing oversold or over bought conditions.
- Divergence strategies take advantage of lower studies and price being out of sync.
