Volume is going to measure the success or the failure of any trading session. The more market participates involved, the more share that have traded hands, the more confident you can be in whatever type of candle that has been developed. 

## What is Volume and Why is it Important to Trading? 

Shows the total number of shares/contracts that have traded in a particular time frame. 

Can help confirm price movement and provide more insight into the "story" the chart is telling us. 

# Volume and Liquidity
The more volume that trades in an asses the more 'liquid' that asset is.

Liquidity refers to the ease of getting buying and selling an asset.

*For every buyer there needs to be a seller*
*For every seller there needs to be a buyer*
