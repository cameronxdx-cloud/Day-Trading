- If you have a market structure with higher highs and lower lows its ~={green}Bullish

![](attachments/Pasted%20image%2020250617114914.png)

- The market will remain bullish as long as we stay in between this higher high and lower low

- The moment body close above or below the last higher low, you will have a shift in the market

![](attachments/Pasted%20image%2020250617115227.png)

- If you close Above the higher high, that mean we created a new higher high
- We must have a new higher low now
- Impossible to have a new higher high without a new higher low

![](attachments/Pasted%20image%2020250617115327.png)

- Then becomes the new higher low
- Snake method, sharp turn 
![](attachments/Pasted%20image%2020250617115501.png)

- **Highest High is the highest point where that market structure is** 
- **Higher Low is the last higher low**
- **Higher high and lower lows are placed at the body of the candle stick, not the wicks**
